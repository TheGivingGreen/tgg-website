YEAR ONE,
IN THE BOOKS.

Last year, St. Louisans packed nine bars in Soulard for our first Charity Pub Golf Crawl. When the scorecards were tallied: $20,634 raised for our nine charity partners, 1.5M+ social impressions, 100% sell-through on every sponsored drink. Not bad for year one. See you October 3.

Got Questions

FAQs

Everything you need to know before you tee off.

TGG is St. Louis's charity pub golf crawl. Nine bars in Soulard become nine holes on a golf course for one Saturday. You carry a physical scorecard, play games at each hole, compete for the lowest score, and raise money for nine local charities at the same time. The most fun you will have on a Saturday and the most painlessly you have ever supported nine great causes.

Every ticket is $45. Your team shirt is your event pass and your souvenir. $15 of that goes directly to the charity you choose at checkout. Your shirt color is your team.

Shirt pickup details will be sent via email in the week before the event. We will have a designated pickup location in Soulard. You must wear your shirt to access venues on event day.

Each hole has a featured sponsored drink and a mini-game or challenge. Order the featured drink and earn -2 strokes. Order any other drink and earn -1. Play the mini-game for -1. Skip a bar entirely and take +2. Carry your scorecard all day across all nine holes.

$15 of your $45 ticket goes directly to your chosen charity. The team shirt you select at checkout is how you choose which one.

The event takes place across nine bars in Soulard, St. Louis. All bars are walkable within the neighborhood. The full course is listed above, and we will send the course map by email before the event.
